B.E. in Computer Engineering

Having a predilection for computers right from an early age, I chose to purse a Bachelor's degree in Computer Engineering from Dwarkadas J. Sanghvi College of Engineering which is affiliated to the University of Mumbai.

In my undergraduate term, I took courses which laid in me a strong technical foundation and made me proficient in computer programming and system development. The courses also helped me understand how to store, manipulate and analyze data to reveal insights, thus introducing me to the field of Data Science. These courses include:

  • Applied Mathematics
  • Structured Programming Approach
  • Object Oriented Programming Methodology
  • Data Structures
  • Analysis of Algorithms
  • Database Management Systems
  • Structured and Object Oriented Analysis and Design
  • Software Engineering
  • Distributed Databases
  • Operations Research
  • Artificial Intelligence
  • Soft Computing
  • Data Warehouse and Mining
  • Human Machine Interaction
  • Parallel & Distributed Systems
  • Machine Learning

I graduated in June, 2019 with a CGPA of 8.86/10.
